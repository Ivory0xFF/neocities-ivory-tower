Me

The site

I made this site in december 2022 after putting it off for a while and come back from time to time to update it

I made it without any frameworks n stuff (except three.js which is loaded through a cdn) and used the neocities editor for a good few months before finally switching to vscode (which is way better-- use the live server extension to host the site locally on a port if you plan on switching too (or maybe nginx))

I try to keep it small, each .html .js and .css file being under 5kb. I also add different things (as an example, around the middle of march 2024, I updated a js script to let me link images from the portfolio directly with anchor links)

I don't really post anywhere except here so if you've somehow found this you're one of the few thousand people (apparently 9,308 on the 22nd of March 2024, according to neocities (update: 10k since sometime during the 18th of May!)) who have seen it up until now!
